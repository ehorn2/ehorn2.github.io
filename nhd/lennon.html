Imagine

← Back to Main Menu

Another anti-war song comes at the latter end of the protest music movement, in 1971. Written by John Lennon, it’s still a frequent player on the radio today. Having multiple interpretations, “Imagine” prospects a world without country boundaries, and the peace that would come from that.

“Imagine there's no countries
It isn't hard to do
Nothing to kill or die for
And no religion too
Imagine all the people living life in peace”

Commonly interpreted as an atheist anthem, “Imagine” also condemns war, and did so at the height of the Vietnam war.
